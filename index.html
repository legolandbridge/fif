FADE IN FILMS releases film and video's for narrative and marketing statements. Our work differs in style but nevertheless remain linked by a single common thread; it explores and questions various aspects of human nature, instincts, and interaction in relation to modern society.



THOMAS

COLLARDEAU

Director / Producer

Often referred to as "my tonton", Thomas is bringing experience to Fade In that no other man could quite claim as his own. His short films were received to wide acclamation across the land of his own imagination, after which he then launched himself into the directing of branding video's. His inclination to success casually leads him to devoted action. Film has been his mission from the word go.
